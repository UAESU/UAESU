WELCOME TO THE SCHOOL OF POSTGRADUATE STUDIES

University of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences (UAES), Umuagwo, Imo State, Nigeria The UAES, with the approval of the National Universities Commission (NUC), has established a School of Postgraduate Studies to run a Master of Science Degree Programme in Agroecology and Food Systems in collaboration with the European Union and Erasmus+ in the 2025/2026 academic session. Candidates may undergo part of the two-year full-time Programme in Partner Universities in France, Austria, Italy, Ghana or Sierra Leone.

SPECIAL MASTER OF SCIENCE DEGREE PROGRAMME

TThe University of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences, Umuagwo, Imo State, Nigeria, in collaboration with the European Union and Erasmus+ invites suitably qualified candidates to apply for an intensive National Universities Commission (NUC) approved full time MSc Programme in Agroecology and Food Systems. Candidates have opportunity to conduct part of their studies in any of the Partner Universities in France, Austria, Italy, Ghana or Sierra Leone.
