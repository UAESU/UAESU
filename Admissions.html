GENERAL ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

Admission is open to candidates who scored a minimum of 150 in the 2025/2026 UTME.

Candidates who did not choose UAES initially can apply after changing institution on JAMB.

Candidates must have 5 O’Level credits in relevant subjects in not more than 2 sittings.Candidates waiting O’ level results can also apply but they will not be considered for admission until their results are available.

HOW TO APPLY

  1. Login to Remita and click on search for biller (University of Agriculture and Environmental Science).
  2. Click on POST UTME (Name of service/purpose) and proceed with your personal details to pay for the application form.
  3. After successful payment, login to University website or click here to fill the application form.
  4. Fill the form completely and correctly and click on submit button.
  5. You will be contacted by the Admission for further directive.
  6. For inquiries and further information
